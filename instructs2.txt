At the beginning of each trial, you will be shown an image similar to the examplesyou just saw. At this point, you should respond by pressing one of the response keys (i.e., the keys labeled 'A' or 'B' on the computer keyboard. You should respond as accurately as possible and you will have up to 2 secondsto make your response. As soon as you respond, the image will disappear, and you will be given feedback. If your response was correct, you will hear a high-pitched tone and see the word "CORRECT" along with name of the category you selected.  Press the space bar to see an example of correct feedback.
